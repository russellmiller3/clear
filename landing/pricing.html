Why flat, not per-seat?

Per-seat pricing kills internal tools.

Every Retool customer story you've heard: "We loved it, then sales doubled and our bill 3x'd. Now we're rebuilding in-house." Per-seat punishes you for the exact thing you bought the tool to enable — getting more people using it.

The Retool problem

You start at $10/seat. RevOps loves it. Marketing wants in. Now finance wants in. Suddenly 60 people across the company touch one Retool dashboard a quarter, and your bill is $7,200/year — for an internal tool that took someone 3 hours to build.

You stop letting people use it. You start gatekeeping. The thing you built to make work easier becomes another bureaucracy.

Common questions

Things people ask before signing up.

What counts as an "agent call"?
Any call to ask claude or a tool-using agent inside one of your deployed apps. The Studio chat (Meph helping you build) doesn't count — that's free on every tier.
What happens if I exceed my agent call quota?
Pay-as-you-go kicks in automatically — $0.02 per call on Team, $0.015 on Business. No app downtime, no email-the-billing-team friction. Your Studio shows the running total so there are no surprises.
Can I cancel or downgrade anytime?
Yes, no contract. Downgrade and your apps keep running until you exceed the new tier's limits — then we email you, you decide what to do. Your data stays yours.
Do you have a free trial of paid tiers?
Yes — 14 days on Team or Business, full features, no credit card. If you don't subscribe at the end, you fall back to Free.
Can I host Clear on my own infrastructure?
On Enterprise. We ship the hosting layer as a Docker bundle that runs in your VPC. Your data, your audit log, your AI key. No traffic leaves your network unless you want it to.
What about the source code I write?
Yours. Every .clear file you write is downloadable, version-controlled, and works on your own machine without us. Worst case: we go away, you keep building.
Why is your AI cheaper than competitors?
Two reasons. We use Anthropic's prompt caching (90% input-token reduction on repeated prefixes) and we default to Haiku 4.5 — 3x cheaper than Sonnet, 94% as good for the structured tasks Clear apps do.
Is there a discount for annual billing?
Yes — pay annually and save 20%. Team becomes $79/month billed yearly, Business becomes $399/month. Email us if you need a custom invoice.
