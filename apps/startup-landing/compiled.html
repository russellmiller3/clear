Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know about Vibe.

Is there a free plan?

Yes. The free plan includes unlimited recordings up to 5 minutes, 25 stored videos, and 720p quality. No credit card required.

Can I record my screen and camera at the same time?

Absolutely. Vibe supports full split-screen and picture-in-picture modes so your team sees your face alongside your work.

How does the AI transcript work?

Every video is automatically transcribed with speaker labels. AI generates chapters and a summary so viewers can skip to exactly what they need.

Does Vibe integrate with Slack and Notion?

Yes. Share videos directly into Slack channels, embed in Notion pages, or paste a link anywhere. The preview unfurls automatically.

Is my data secure?

All videos are encrypted in transit and at rest. Business plans include SSO, audit logs, and data residency options. SOC 2 Type II certified.
